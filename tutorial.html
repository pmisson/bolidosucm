Fire ball detection program: Bolidos UCM

Hi! This is application to help on the detection of meteors in tracking station Complutense University.

The application is really simple. It loads a photo from Station at Observatorio UCM and asks you this question: Can you see a meteor?

You will have 3 possible answers:

  • Yes,
  • No, and
  • I don't know

All you have to do is to click in one of the three possible answers and you will be done.
